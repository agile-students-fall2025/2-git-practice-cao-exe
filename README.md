[Learnings from two years of using AI tools for software engineering](https://newsletter.pragmaticengineer.com/p/two-years-of-using-ai).

The article explores how AI tools have rapidly transformed software development over the past two years. AI usage in coding has developed from simple code completion to entirely autonomous coding agents. Although the entire impact on effectiveness and productivity is not certain, the author highlights that AI coding assistants have indeed increased the speed of software delivery in organizations. One especially interesting idea that the author introduces is the concept of assigning your AI coding assistant a mental model of a teammate that is over-confident, stubborn, well-read but inexperienced, and always trying to help. This helps us realize that AI tools are not perfect and we need to carefully evaluate their suggestions, just like we would with human teammates.
